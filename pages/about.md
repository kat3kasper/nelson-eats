Nelson Eats is a simple guide to happy hour specials in Nelson, British Columbia.

Nelson has more restaurants per capita than cities like San Francisco and Vancouver. We want to make it easy for locals and visitors to find the best deals on food and drinks around town.

## How it works

We research happy hour deals from restaurant websites, social media, and direct outreach. Each deal is marked as verified or unverified so you know how fresh the info is.

## Want to update your listing?

If you're a restaurant owner and want to update your deals, [let us know](/pages/submit.html). Listings are always free.
