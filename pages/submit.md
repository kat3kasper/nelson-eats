Want to add or update your restaurant's happy hour deals? We'd love to hear from you.

## Restaurant owners

Email us at [katelyn@kasperweb.com](mailto:katelyn@kasperweb.com) with:

- Your restaurant name
- Happy hour days and times
- What's on special (food, drinks, or both)
- Any seasonal changes we should know about

Listings on Nelson Eats are completely free.

## Spotted a deal?

If you know about a happy hour that's not listed, send us a tip and we'll look into it.
